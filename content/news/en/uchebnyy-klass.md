title: On-site training class opens at the plant
lead: A classroom for occupational-safety training and qualification upgrades for production staff.
minutes: 3
---

A dedicated training class for 16 students has opened on plant premises. CNC operators,
welders, riggers, forklift drivers and QC inspectors — everyone whose work requires
periodic re-certification in occupational safety and industrial safety — will study here.

The room is equipped with a projector, training simulators, sample blanks and tools,
plus stands for hands-on practice. Training programs are aligned with NAKS,
Rostechnadzor and cover both current GOST/SNiP standards and internal plant standards.

Opening the training class is part of a broader staff retention and development program.
In parallel, the plant works with St. Petersburg College of Electronics and Polytech
on targeted-training programs: students join paid internships and often stay on
after graduation.
