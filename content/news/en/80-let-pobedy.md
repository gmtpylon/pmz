During the Great Patriotic War Pushkin Metallurgical Plant (back then «Detskoye Selo
repair works») switched to producing for the Leningrad front: repairing bitumen sprayers
and concrete mixers, manufacturing steel parts for defense needs, sending repair crews
to the front lines.

For the 80th anniversary of Victory the plant team has reached out to our veteran
colleagues and their families. Their labor, their ability to work double and triple
shifts under shortages of everything — is part of the history we cherish and lean on
whenever we say: «we have made heavy metal since 1919».

Happy Victory Day. Thank you to those thanks to whom it exists.
