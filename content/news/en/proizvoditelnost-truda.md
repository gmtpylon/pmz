Pushkin Metallurgical Plant has joined the national "Labour Productivity" project.
The project helps companies produce more without extra investment, through smarter
organisation of work.

As part of it, the plant ran a business game on the basics of lean manufacturing.
Using simple models, the team worked through how losses are cut at the workplace,
how a production flow is built and why a well-organised area can noticeably raise
output.

The plant applies these principles across its own areas — from the rolling mill to
the thermal-diffusion galvanizing line — gradually removing unnecessary steps and
shortening order lead times.
