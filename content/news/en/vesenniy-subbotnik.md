Ahead of the May holidays, employees of the Pushkin Machine-Building Plant took part
in a spring clean-up. The team helped tidy the area around the plant and public
spaces in the town of Pushkin after winter.

They cleared last year's leaves and litter and put lawns and green areas in order.
Workers from different parts of the plant joined in — from the shop floor to the
engineering services.

Taking part in town clean-ups is a normal part of life at the plant: we work in
Pushkin and want the town around us to be clean and well kept.
